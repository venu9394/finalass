Guidelines:
  • If you select Half day applicable then mentioned date should be matched with FROM date or TO date OR else it will be considered as full day leave.
  • If you select Comp-off applicable then mentioned date should be matched with week-off or public holiday or else it won't be considered.
  • If any Public holiday (OR) Weak off days in between days it will be considered.
  • Note: For further information please contact HR.
